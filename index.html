About Me

I was born and raised in the city of San Francisco. I moved to Chicago for college. I graduated from Wheaton College in 2017 and then moved to Pilsen and started working at the local charter high school. I am so grateful for this opportunity to change careers! I feel so blessed to be able to prioritize what is truly important to me, like my recent marriage and my health and my church family. Thank you for teaching me! If you ever want any teaching feedback or advice, please let me know because I would love to give back and make the program even better.
